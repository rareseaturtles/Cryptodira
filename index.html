Mission & Utility:
CryptoDira (DIRA) is a Solana-based token dedicated to supporting sea turtle conservation and broader Earth-cleaning initiatives through online engagement. As a merit-based digital asset, DIRA rewards users for completing online tasks—such as signing the pledge, voting, trying their luck, and following on X while funding conservation efforts. Use DIRA to stake, engage in community activities, all while fostering a cleaner planet. Our goal? Seamless online participation, sustainable growth, and less trash.
Tasks for Earning DIRA:
- Sign the pledge on our website (e.g., 10 DIRA).
- Vote on our platform at diramocracy.netlify.app (e.g., 15 DIRA).
- Try your luck for rewards at turtletreasure.netlify.app (e.g., up to 50 DIRA).
- Follow us on X at https://x.com/rareseaturtles (e.g., 5 DIRA).
Tasks are verified via platform interactions and require a Phantom wallet connection.
Future Expansion:
We’re exploring the introduction of physical tasks, such as cleaning up trash or organizing community cleanups, to further engage our community in real-world environmental impact—stay tuned for updates!
